About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where you face endless waves of monsters in a fight for survival. As a lone hero with unique abilities, your mission is to survive as long as possible while defeating hordes of increasingly difficult enemies.

The game features roguelike elements with permanent progression, allowing you to become stronger with each playthrough. Collect experience points to level up and choose from a variety of upgrades and new abilities to customize your character's build.

Game Features

  • Endless waves of diverse monsters
  • Multiple character classes with unique abilities
  • Roguelike progression system
  • Dozens of upgrades and power-ups
  • Challenging boss encounters
  • Increasing difficulty as you survive longer

How to Play

Movement: Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map.

Attacks: Your character attacks automatically when enemies are in range.

Level Up: Collect experience orbs from defeated enemies to level up and choose new upgrades.

Survive: Stay alive as long as possible while defeating increasingly difficult waves of monsters.

Strategy Tips

Build Synergy: Choose upgrades that complement each other for maximum effectiveness.

Kiting: Keep moving to avoid being surrounded by enemies.

Prioritize Threats: Focus on eliminating the most dangerous enemies first.

Resource Management: Collect health pickups when you're low on health, not when you're at full health.

Challenges

Survival Mode: How long can you survive against endless waves?

Boss Rush: Face increasingly difficult boss monsters back-to-back.

Limited Upgrades: Complete the game with restricted upgrade choices.

Speed Run: Defeat a certain number of enemies as quickly as possible.
